RAF Gaza Spy Flights

Raw data Github

Since December 2023, the British Royal Air Force have repeatedly flown Shadow R1 intelligence gathering planes from RAF Akrotiri in Cyprus over the occupied Gaza Strip. The flights leave Akrotiri, turning off their transponders some 100 miles from the Gaza Strip, before returning several hours later. Intelligence gathered during these flights has been shared with the Israeli government, under the notion of 'locating the remaining hostages.' However, no public clarification has been given as to the nature of the intelligence being shared, with concerns being raised that it may be used to assist with war crimes during Israel's genocidal onslaught in Gaza. This website aims to automatically collect data on these flights from publicly available sources (primarily adsb.fi), and compile it in one place. The site has been running consistently since 2025-07-14 and should accurately document all flights since that date.
